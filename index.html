About Us

Logic Legion was founded in 2025. We combine computer science, mechanical design, and strategy to build competitive robots. We value teamwork and sportsmanship, and strive to become the best we can be.

Our Philosophy

We strive to become better teammates through collaboration, teamwork and coordination.

Teamwork

Competition Goals

The Robot

Our robot for the 2025-2026 Decode season.

Decode Robot

Our robot will have an intake that uses boot wheels coupled with a double rhino wheel outtake.

Drive
Mecanum Drivetrain
Shooter
goBILDA Rhino Wheels • goBILDA 435 RPM Motors
Intake
goBILDA Boot Wheels • goBILDA 1150 RPM Motor
Control
REV Control Hub • REV Expansion Hub

Specifications

  • Weight: 18 kg
  • Battery: 13v LiPo
  • Motors: 8 goBILDA 5203 Series Yellow Jackets
  • Sensors: Dead Wheel Odometry
  • Chassis: Custom goBILDA U-Channel Chassis
